Delicious Chocolate Cookie Recipe

Second Page

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cups of brown sugar
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 egg yolks at room temp
  • 2 tbsp of corn syrup
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup of white chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup of dark chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F on the middle tray
  2. Use room temp bowl
  3. Put butter in bowl and beat to 45 seconds to 1 minute
  4. Add egg yolks and mix
  5. Add vanilla and corn syrup and mix
  6. add dry ingredients and stir
  7. Add Chocolate chips and fold in
  8. Add 12 small balls of cookie dough and add to 2 trays
  9. Bake for 10 minutes
  10. remove top tray and move the second in its place for 1 minute
  11. Leave cookies to cool for 20 minutes
