Chocolate Raspberry Loaf
Previous Page Landing Page

Wet Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 2 large room temp eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of lemon zest

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

Add-ins and Decor

  • 6-8 raspberries
  • 1 1/2 cup of raspberries
  • 2 tsp of flour
  • 1/2 cup of white chocolate chips

Glaze

  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 6 tsp of milk
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 190C/375F.
  1. Grease and line a 22 x 12 x 7 cm loaf pan.
  2. toss raspberries with flour, if frozen put in the freezer to prevent thawing.
  3. put the butter in a heatproof bowl and microwave until melted. cool for a minute and whisk in sugar. Add other wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  4. Sprinkle dry ingredients across the surface and mix in with a spatula. Stop mixing until flour is incoporated.
  5. Add the white chocolate and raspberries then mix briefy. Try not to squish fresh raspberries, for frozen raspberries mix quickly before the batter chills.
  6. Scrape the batter into the pan and level the surface. Place extra raspberries on the surface.
  7. Bake for 40 minutes and cover loosely with foil and bake for 35 minutesfor fresh/40 for frozen raspberries.
  8. Cool for 5 minutes and transfer to rack. Allow to cool before glazing.

Glaze Directions

  1. Mix glaze ingredients until smooth.
  2. Put the rack on tray or a plate. Drizzle over cake, scrape up glaze from the plate and continue.
  3. Leave for 20 minutes to let glaze crust and then cut into thick slices.
