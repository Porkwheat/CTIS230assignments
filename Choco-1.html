Cute Mini Chocolate Cakes

Ingredients

    1 cup plain all purpose flour 1/3 cup of cocoa powder 3/4 tsp of baking powder 1 cup of white sugar 1/2 tsp of salt 1 large room temp egg 1/2 cup of room temp milk 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of boiling water
  • 1 tsp of instant coffee

    • Frosting ingredients and Decor

    • 1 cup of unsalted butter
    • 4 cups of powdered sugar
    • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
    • 5 tbsp of milk
    • Dark chocolate
    • Raspberries and rosemary sprigs

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F
    2. Butter 40/28.5 x 2.5cm cm tray then line with parchment paper
    3. Whisk in dry ingredients until combined
    4. Mix in wet ingredients until combined. Dissolve coffee in hot water then add to batter.
    5. Add to pan and bake for 13 minutes
    6. Cool for 10 minutes then lift up by parchment paper and cool for 30 minutes
    7. Refrigerate for 1 hour

    Chocolate Buttercream

    1. Beat butter for 3 minutes on high. Add powered sugar and then beat on low, continue adding.
    2. beat for 3 minutes after all powered sugar has been added, add cocoa powder, milk and vanilla, beat for 3 minutes until fluffy.
    3. Transfer to piping bag.

    Assembling

    1. Flip cake on cutting board. Cut out 21 x 6cm sizes.
    2. Pipe frosting on layers around the sides and on the top.
    3. Smooth the frosting with a spatula or knife.
    4. Decorate with chocolate shards, raspberries and rosemary sprigs.
